About us

Passion and commitment

We are passionate about cooking but that's not all. We are a community based social enterprise which aims to reduce health inequalities associated with food and nutrition. We provide training and volunteering opportunities for those who may find themselves marginalised and excluded and we provide access to fresh nutritious food at affordable prices in low income communities.
